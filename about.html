My Path:

I didn’t grow up knowing exactly what I wanted to do—but I was always trying something new. One year I was writing a book. The next, I was choreographing dances, learning to code, or designing posters for fun.

Whether it was tennis, drawing, or building something from scratch, I followed whatever sparked my interest.

That mindset stayed with me. Studying Information Science and Advertising/Public Relations let me channel that creativity into work that blends design, storytelling, and systems thinking. Now, I’m most excited by problems that don’t have clear answers because they push me to learn, adapt, and make something meaningful.

My Approach:
  1. Follow curiosity, even when it leads somewhere unexpected.
  2. Build with both structure and soul.
  3. Design things that make people feel understood.

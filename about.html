Welcome to my website! I’m Krista, a creative technologist passionate about product design, project management, and building thoughtful digital experiences — studying Advertising and Public Relations with a double minor in Shuford Program in Entrepreneurship and Information Science at UNC Chapel Hill.

You can probably find me: Reading a book

My Path:

I didn’t grow up knowing exactly what I wanted to do, but I was always trying something new. One year I was writing a book. The next, I was choreographing dances, learning to code, or designing posters for fun.

Whether it was tennis, drawing, or building something from scratch, I followed whatever sparked my interest.

That mindset stayed with me. Studying Information Science and Advertising/Public Relations lets me channel that creativity into work that blends design, storytelling, and systems thinking. Now, I’m most excited by problems that don’t have clear answers because they push me to learn, adapt, and make something meaningful.

My Approach:
  1. Follow curiosity, even when it leads somewhere unexpected.
  2. Build with both structure and soul.
  3. Design things that make people feel understood.
The people around me shape the spaces I call home.

It’s not just places that define who I am, it’s the people I’ve shared them with. I’ve grown through conversations over late-night dinners, collaborations with teammates, moments of stillness with close friends, and spontaneous adventures that turned into lasting memories.

Every space feels more alive, more meaningful, when it’s built on connection.

From creative projects to casual weekends, I carry those relationships with me. They’ve taught me empathy, resilience, and the importance of showing up—not just in work, but in life.
